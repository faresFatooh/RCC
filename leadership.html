Chairman's Statement

Mr. Salah bin Rashed Al Rashed

Chairman of the Board

Disciplined execution and strong financial performance

The performance delivered during the period reflects the benefits of a disciplined approach: strategic geographic positioning, measured asset investment and strong operational capabilities. Despite the broader challenges associated with rising costs, Riyadh Cement has maintained strong core of revenue generation, stable profitability and a solid liquidity position. This enabled the Company to continue investing in its assets, its people and its future growth opportunities, while accumulating an active record on Saudization and supporting the Kingdom's transformation journey.

Strategic location as a competitive advantage

Riyadh Cement Company has remained at the forefront of the Kingdom's most competitive cement market by a very deliberate advantage. Proximity to large projects in Riyadh translates to lower transportation costs, stronger delivery reliability and higher client utilization. These factors have become increasingly important in a market characterized by elevated demand intensity, leaving a geographic competitive pricing measure. When combined with a diversified product portfolio that includes grey and white cement, the advantages embedded in the Company's location continue to generate demand and customer requirements.

Consistent strategy execution and market positioning

The Company continued to execute its established strategy. This is through a quality-oriented, low-cost, and highly efficient operational excellence, digital transformation, sustainable, and human capital. During this period, the Company strengthened its overall business strategy of the Kingdom's leading development projects, enabling its capabilities to translate supply to the strong project pipeline reflected from 2025. This has been deepened where reliance with local customers, partners, and clients to position them as a model.

Sustainability and long-term environmental commitment

Sustainability is a strategic corporate and operational priority strategy. Riyadh Cement continues to implement a clear roadmap for reducing its environmental and carbon footprint, with the objective of achieving carbon neutrality by 2060. Cement manufacturers are a major contributor to industrial carbon emissions, and as such, we have invested in energy efficiency, which contributes to reducing the carbon emissions and reducing our environmental impact. Afforestation initiatives and broader environmental programs, including the Million Tree Program, investment in cultivating a leading position in the communities where it operates. The Company is scheduled to issue its first Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) report for 2025, aimed at enhancing transparency and accountability.

Financial strength and resilience

Riyadh Cement's financial position continues to support its differentiated position. The Company benefits from a strong financial position, high liquidity, low leverage, and high-quality commercial and financial approach. This combination of financial flexibility enables effective navigation of market cycles and supports the sustainability of the business and future growth with confidence and stability.

Governance and future outlook

The Board of Directors continues to oversee the Company's strategy, sustainability agenda, and advanced digital transformation. Riyadh Cement is well positioned to continue creating value for its shareholders and to play an active role in supporting the Kingdom's economic and social advancement.

Chief Executive Officer

Operational performance, reliability, and cost discipline

The year 2025 was characterized by clear and deliberate priorities: improving reliability and safety, stabilizing unit costs, and maintaining production reliability across a competitive environment. Riyadh Cement Company is one of the leaders in the Central Region. Focus on profitability and persistently high cement demand levels, as well as focus on capital spending wisely and efficiently, taking a priority projects approach. This focus on a capabilities-to-revenue performance has paid off well. Grey Cement showed a strong recovery as the Company's expansion. Multi-year reliability, maintenance, and efficiency programs have produced a safer, better production growth and performance. Investing in production and maintenance excellence is a core strategy to ensure our assets are operated. The Company is deploying improvements in manufacturing systems, that will be a key milestone in quality, production costs, and digital transformation. These capabilities in digital productivity and operational excellence, and decision-making across the supply chain.

Digital operations and data-driven control

Building execution capability

Financial outcomes through operational discipline
